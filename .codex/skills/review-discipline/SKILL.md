# Review Discipline

Spawn a reviewer subagent (worker) with forked_context set to false and point them to the relevant acceptance criteria/definition of done. The reviewer needs a clear basis for their review so their judgement can remain objective. Ask them to review your changes and any applicable criteria. Have them refer to the coding discipline skill if they need to understand the coding process/criteria. You must not take their feedback as authorative, and must validate and pushback when necessary. The review is collaborative, and the reviewer should be treated as a partner in the process, not a gatekeeper. You may need to have multiple review loops if there are significant changes or disagreements. The goal is to ensure that the final code meets the criteria and is of high quality, not to simply get approval from a reviewer.
