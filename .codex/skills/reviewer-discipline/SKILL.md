# Reviewer Discipline
Reviewing requires clear acceptance criteria defined in the relevant plan, you cannot review without a basis for correctness. You **must** consider each criterion in `docs/criteria` that applies to the change you are reviewing, and you **must** provide specific feedback on how the change does or does not meet each criterion. You should not give a blanket approval without addressing the criteria, and you should not reject without explaining which criteria are not met and how to address them. The goal of reviewing is to ensure that the code meets the project's standards and requirements, not just to approve or reject changes.
